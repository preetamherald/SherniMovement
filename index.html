What Is Sherni Movement

Sherni Movement is an initiative by our team aiming to encourage girls to take safety measures, giving them a fighting chance and ultimately make a better society for all of us.

We are creating a community, using an App
Where people can report signs of distress and We as a community can take actions to prevent them from going bigger and to stop such crimes from ever happening.
Providing Support to Those in Need.

A Community consisting of peoples who want this world to change.
Those who are willing to actually stand up to something and take actions which are necessary.
